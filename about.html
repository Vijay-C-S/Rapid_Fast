About GetUpdated

Your one-stop destination for staying ahead in the digital world

Our Story

GetUpdated was born out of a simple idea — to create a single platform where people can find everything they need to stay informed and grow in the digital age. In a world overflowing with information, we recognized the need for a curated, trustworthy source that brings together tech news, career opportunities, money-saving deals, and in-depth AI & tech blogs.

Founded in 2025 and based in Bangalore, India, we started as a small passion project and have quickly grown into a go-to platform that helps thousands of users every day. Our team is passionate about technology, career growth, and helping people make smarter decisions — whether it's choosing the right AI tool, landing a dream job, or saving money on everyday purchases.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals with timely, accurate, and actionable information that helps them advance in their careers, stay updated with technology and AI, and make smarter financial decisions every single day.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted platform for job seekers, tech enthusiasts, AI explorers, and anyone looking to grow personally and financially in the digital economy.
