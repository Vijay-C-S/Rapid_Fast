Mullanpur Pitch Report and Weather Impact

The Mullanpur surface is still relatively new in IPL terms, but we already have a useful pattern. The wicket has shown good pace and carry for stroke-makers, especially in the first half. Teams that start well in powerplay can post strong totals here.

At the same time, this is not a pure batting road. As the ball gets older, spinners and bowlers with smart pace variation find purchase. That makes middle-over control and death-over execution critical. A side that bowls one bad over less than the opponent can decide this match.

Weather is expected to stay clear and warm, with no major rain threat. So we are likely to get a full game without interruption. Dew could still play a role later in the night, which is one reason captains may prefer chasing after winning the toss.

Ground-level takeaway for fantasy players and prediction followers:

  • Top-order batters: High value because of true bounce early on.
  • Wrist spin and quality finger spin: Useful in overs 7-15.
  • Death pacers: Reliable wicket source in final overs.
  • All-rounders: Big impact due to dual-point opportunity.

Head-to-Head Record and Form Context

Historically, GT and PBKS have stayed neck-and-neck in their direct contests. Neither side has had complete dominance, which again supports the idea that this is a tactical matchup rather than a talent mismatch.

When two teams are evenly matched, these three points become match-deciding:

  • Powerplay batting: Who handles the new ball better.
  • Middle-over squeeze: Which side controls singles and boundaries.
  • Death-over discipline: Wides, yorkers, and pressure handling.

PBKS can gain control if Chahal and Arshdeep execute at key transition points. GT can take over if Gill and Buttler set a powerplay base and Rashid shuts down scoring momentum later.

Top Player Battles That Can Decide the Match

1. Shubman Gill vs Arshdeep Singh

This is a classic control-vs-control duel. Gill likes to settle and then accelerate. Arshdeep's angle and seam presentation can challenge him early. If Arshdeep wins this phase, PBKS gain immediate leverage.

2. Jos Buttler vs Yuzvendra Chahal

Buttler is destructive against pace once set, but quality leg-spin in the middle overs can test his rhythm. Chahal's role is not only to take wickets but to force GT to recalculate tempo.

3. Shreyas Iyer vs Rashid Khan

Shreyas anchors and rotates well, but Rashid can choke strike with tight lines and subtle pace changes. This mini battle could decide whether PBKS reach 175+ or settle for a lower total.

4. Marcus Stoinis vs GT Death Bowling

If Stoinis is in by over 14, he can tilt the game quickly with a late burst. GT need Siraj and Rabada to deny slot balls at the death.
